Isolation

PiP is a chromeless, rounded, always-on-top WebviewWindow pointed at youtube.com — full site, your login, no embed blocks; draggable, snaps to corners. it merely looks native-PiP. the main window's CSP (zero remote content) does not change — that isolation is the design.

The interrupt

on needs-you: eval video.pause() in the tv webview + inject the strip. ⇥ approves through the same command the desktop uses; resolve-anywhere clears it and resumes. one auto-pause per 30s max.

Scope

side-quest after M3 (approvals must exist first). no playlists, no media-center ambitions — a webview with an interrupt strip. if youtube's DOM drifts, pause degrades gracefully; the strip still shows. spec: design/tv.md.
